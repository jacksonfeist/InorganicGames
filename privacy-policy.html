Privacy Policy

Inorganic Games LLC — TikTok Mini Games
Last updated: June 22, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Inorganic Games LLC ("Inorganic Games," "we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with our game (the "Game"), which is made available through TikTok as a Mini Game / Mini App.

We do not operate our own account system, server-side database of player profiles, or first-party analytics for the Game. The Game runs entirely within the TikTok platform, and most data processing associated with playing the Game is performed by TikTok, not by us directly.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not directly collect, store, or maintain:

  • Names, email addresses, or other contact information;
  • User accounts, usernames, or passwords specific to the Game;
  • Player save data or progress data on our own servers;
  • Location data;
  • Payment information.

3. Information Collected Through TikTok

Because the Game operates inside TikTok's platform, TikTok itself collects and processes information about your use of the Game as part of its normal platform operation. This may include device information, usage data, and identifiers TikTok uses across its services, including for advertising purposes. This collection is governed by TikTok's own Privacy Policy, not this one. We encourage you to review TikTok's Privacy Policy to understand what TikTok collects and how it is used.

We do not have access to most of this platform-level data and do not control how TikTok collects or uses it.

4. Advertising

The Game displays ads served through TikTok's advertising network. As part of delivering ads, TikTok's ad systems may process device identifiers, usage information, and other data in accordance with TikTok's own advertising and privacy policies. We do not independently collect, receive, or use data for ad targeting — ad delivery and any related data processing is handled entirely by TikTok.

If TikTok offers settings to limit ad personalization (such as an ad preferences or "Why this ad" control), those settings are managed through TikTok's platform, not through us.

5. How Information Is Used

To the extent any information passes through our systems at all (for example, aggregated or non-identifying technical information made available to us by TikTok for the purpose of operating the Game), we use it only to operate, maintain, and improve the Game. We do not sell personal information, and we do not have a mechanism to directly identify individual players outside of what TikTok's platform provides to developers generally.

6. Data Sharing

We do not share player data with third parties, because we do not collect or hold player data directly. Any data sharing that occurs as part of normal advertising or platform operation happens between you, TikTok, and TikTok's advertising partners, under TikTok's own policies.

7. Children's Privacy

We do not operate an age-verification system for the Game, and the Game does not require a separate account, so we have no independent way to verify the age of individual players. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from players of any age, because we do not operate a data collection system of our own for the Game.

We are aware that platforms like TikTok may have users under the age of 13, and TikTok maintains its own policies and protections, including restrictions on personalized advertising to minors, under its own Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. Those protections operate at the platform level and are TikTok's responsibility, not ours.

If you are a parent or guardian and have concerns about your child's use of the Game, we encourage you to review TikTok's parental controls and privacy settings, and you may also contact us at the email below.

8. Data Retention

Because we do not maintain a database of player information, we have no player data of our own to retain or delete. Any data associated with your use of the Game that exists on TikTok's systems is retained and deleted according to TikTok's own data retention practices.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding personal data processed about you (such as access, correction, or deletion requests). Because TikTok, not us, holds the data generated by your use of the Game, these requests are generally best directed to TikTok directly through their platform tools or privacy policy. If you contact us, we will do what we reasonably can to assist or direct you to the appropriate channel, but we may not be able to access or modify data held by TikTok on our behalf.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, for example to reflect changes in how the Game operates or changes in TikTok's platform policies that affect this description. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
